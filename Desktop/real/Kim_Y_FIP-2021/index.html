Our Story

Get this, in 1911, Joshua Cutex Challenged Harry Houdini to be handcuffed, chained and escape from a barrel of Cutex ale at the Empire Theatre, Leeds. Houdini was teetotal and the barrel, wheeled over by lads from Cutex Brewery, was full to the brim. Harry failed to escape, but was rescued. At least you can get on the outside of a Cutex right now.
